This happens when the phone volume itself is low.
To increase the volume during conversation, while on call user can press the volume key on the phone to increse the volume.

Instead of incoming call announcement, ringing is heard in Bose Frames
This happens when the phone is on silent mode.
User should always keep phone's silent mode OFF while using ECA.

Along with incoming caller announcement, Nod head to answer, shake head to decline is not announced(when bose frames are connected.)
  1. If the phone is connected to different bluetooth device previously, this could happen. To solve this problem switch OFF and switch ON the bluetooth.
  2. Also this could happen when bose frames are not connected properly. To connect bose frames properly connect them through Bose Connect App.
