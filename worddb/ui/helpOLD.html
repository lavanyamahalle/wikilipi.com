1. Announcer Incoming
    Without seeing on the screen you can hear, who is calling you!!.
    For that:
    • Allow Ekalipi Call Announcer to access your contacts
    • Allow Ekalipi Call Announcer to make and manage phone calls
  2. Announcer Outgoing
    When you call to someone it announces name of the person
    For that:
    • Allow Ekalipi Call Announcer to access your contacts
    • Allow Ekalipi Call Announcer to make and manage phone calls
EDIT PREFIX / SUFFIX
  • Click to the EDIT PREFIX/SUFFIX Option
  • It does much more!
On the screen below you can add greetings:
  • before the name is played (called a prefix) or
  • after the name is played (called a suffix)
  • For example, for incoming calls it can say “Call From (köl f-röm)” before the name in English or “फोन करित आहे (fon kareŧ āhλ)”after the name in Marathi.
  • outgoing calls, it can say “Now calling (nå köleŋø)” and then the person’s name.
    • Be creative and have fun with it.
