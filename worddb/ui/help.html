EDIT PREFIX / SUFFIX
Optionally add your custom greetings before and/or after the person’s name is announced. The greeting needs to be written in our Ekalipi phonetic script using the custom onscreen keyboard. For further details, please visit our websites ekalipi.org and ekalipi.com

Examples: For incoming calls you can have it say :
  • English : "Call From" (köl f-röm) before the announcement of the name
  • Marathi : “फोन करित आहे” (fon kareŧ āhλ) after the announcement of the name
For outgoing calls have it say :
  • English : "Now calling" (nå köleŋø) and then the person's name.
Note - For sample greetings in other languages please visit www.ekalipi.com/eca.html
Tip – In crowded places, it's hard to know whose phone is ringing. If you add your own name in the greeting e.g. Hey satyakala” (hλ saŧ-yakalā); when you get a call, you and others will know that you are receiving the call and won’t have to check their phones.
Be creative with the announcements, have fun!!

This capability is very valuable if you need to avoid the distraction of looking or typing on your phone.

ANDROID SETTING

  • AUTO START
    • If your Call Announcer App does not start automatically and you are using a custom OS like ViVO or Redmi, please tap on this option.

  • BATTERY OPTIMISATION
    • Are you using one of the latest Android phones (version 7.0 or above)? If so, you may notice that the app sometimes closes automatically. To prevent this, tap on this tab.
    If you are using Android 9 or above, you need to set battery optimization to Don't Optimize a second time, this time in Phone Settings. On the phone go to Settings --> Battery --> Battery Optimization.
    Note- This may increase your battery consumption slightly.

  • SILENT ON KEY PRESS
    • This setting allows you to stop the announcement of a caller's name by pressing the volume up/down button on your phone.

SYNC NOW
  • SYNC TO PHONE :
    • Updates the phonetic names in your contact list from the phonetic names stored in our central dictionary.
  • SYNC TO SERVER :
    • Provides a list of missing phonetic names to the central Ekalipi dictionary.
You will notice that we have already populated the phonetic name for a number of your contacts. We do this by utilizing a server based dictionary containing the most commonly known names.
  • When you initially install the app, we will load any missing names to our server. Our team of dedicated professionals will provide the missing phonetic names within a week .
  • When you sync your phone your contact list will be automatically updated with the Phonetic name.
    • This process allows our Call Announcer to pronounce names correctly (unlike other Call Announcers)
